A brief introduction

My name is Will and I'm an incoming Masters of Public Health Student at Yale University (Epidemiology of Microbial Diseases). Before that, I was an undergraduate student at McGill University in Canada, majoring in Bioengineering.

What is this website about?

This website will serve as a collection of my academic projects, freelance writing, as well as my hobbies in food and travelling. Should be pretty self-explanatory if you look at the navigation tabs!

This website looks like its from the '90s.

Have you seen the original Amazon website? I'm a huge fan of retro designs (nerd alert) too. Also, I'm still learning the very basics of HTML/CSS; I'm making it a point to not use the ready-made website development tools out there. Hopefully, my future projects will catch up more to the modern era, but I think I will keep this page as a reminder of my humble beginnings. Credit also goes to Professor Qixuan Chen at Columbia, from whom I took inspiration for this website design.

Food and Travel?

Who doesn't like food and travel? I'm very privileged to have travelled to quite a few places thanks to the support of my parents. Although commiting to research probably means that I will be living a more frugual lifestyle, I hope to pursue travel/food writing as a side hobby. I'm very interested in the intersection between culture and ingredients, and creating recipes for a student who is always short on time.
